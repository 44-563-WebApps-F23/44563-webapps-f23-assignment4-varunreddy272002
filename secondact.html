NURSE.

Little Nora, poor dear, had no other mother but me.

NORA.

And if my little ones had no other mother, I am sure you would—What nonsense I am talking! _[Opens the box.]_ Go in to them. Now I must—. You will see tomorrow how charming I shall look.

NURSE.

I am sure there will be no one at the ball so charming as you, ma’am.

_[Goes into the room on the left.]_
